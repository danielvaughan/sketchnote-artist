Title Text: J.Y. Park's "Fever": The Reality of a Crush
Central Metaphor: A vibrant dream bubble popping on a dull background.
Visual Hierarchy:
Header (Big Text): The Humorous Chasm Between Infatuation and Reality
Sub-Headers (Medium Text):
- Instant Spark
- Grand Illusions
- Awkward Reality Check
- The Punchline of Disappointment
Details (Small Text):
- Instant Spark:
    - Mundane routine interrupted by a beautiful new neighbor.
    - Instant infatuation upon receiving rice cakes.
    - Thrown into a state of high excitement.
- Grand Illusions:
    - Elevator fantasy: Sensual dance in red light.
    - Balcony fantasy: Vaudeville stage performance.
    - Traditional setting fantasy: Pounding rice dough with exaggerated passion.
    - "Wild internal imagination" taking over.
- Awkward Reality Check:
    - Contrasting polite interactions with internal wild imagination.
    - J.Y. Park's signature facial expressions and physical comedy.
    - Humor from desperation of the crush.
- The Punchline of Disappointment:
    - Return to the elevator setting.
    - Anticipation of another fantasy when lights flicker.
    - Lights simply turn back on; nothing happens.
    - "Disappointed and awkward in the silence."
Iconography Suggestions:
- For "Instant Spark": A heart bursting, a lightbulb turning on, traditional rice cakes.
- For "Grand Illusions": A thought bubble, disco ball, stage curtains, traditional Korean Hanbok, exaggerated dance poses.
- For "Awkward Reality Check": A question mark, J.Y. Park's comically expressive face, a plain elevator button.
- For "The Punchline of Disappointment": A deflated balloon, a sad face, an empty thought bubble.
- General: Music notes (swing-jazz style), a "fever" thermometer (comedic).
Mood: Playful, comedic, slightly melancholic (at the end).
